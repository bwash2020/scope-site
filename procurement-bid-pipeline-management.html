Monitoring

Curated Procurement
Monitoring

After onboarding, Scope monitors selected procurement sources based on your business profile, NAICS/NIGP codes, certifications, service area, keywords, target agencies, and opportunity preferences. Matching opportunities are filtered, scored, and organized into your bid pipeline for review.

Automation helps find and organize opportunities. Scope provides review, judgment, and coordination.

How Monitoring Works

  1. 01
    Client completes onboarding profile — business type, codes, certifications, geography, and target agencies.
  2. 02
    Scope monitors selected procurement sources based on your package and preferences.
  3. 03
    Opportunities are matched by NAICS/NIGP codes, keywords, certifications, geography, and deadlines.
  4. 04
    Matches are scored — High Fit, Medium Fit, or Low Fit — before reaching your pipeline.
  5. 05
    Scope reviews top matches for fit, urgency, and next steps before sending to the client.
  6. 06
    Approved opportunities move into the bid tracker with deadlines, addenda, Q&A dates, and submission tracking.
  7. 07
    Weekly action list delivered with priorities, upcoming deadlines, and required next steps.
